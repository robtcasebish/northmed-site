GLP-1 FAQ

Still have questions? Our care team is available 24/7.

Start Assessment

Most patients with a BMI over 27 with a weight-related health condition, or a BMI over 30, may qualify. Your physician will review your full medical history during the consultation to determine eligibility.

Semaglutide (Ozempic/Wegovy) is a GLP-1 receptor agonist. Tirzepatide (Mounjaro/Zepbound) is a dual GIP and GLP-1 agonist, which in clinical trials produced greater average weight loss. Your physician will help you choose based on your health profile.

Compounded medications are prepared by Health Canada registered pharmacies and undergo quality testing. While they are not brand-name products, they contain the same active ingredient. Your physician will discuss the options and help you make an informed choice.

The most common side effects are nausea, constipation, and mild fatigue, particularly during dose escalation. These typically resolve within a few weeks. Your care team will guide you through the titration schedule to minimize discomfort.
