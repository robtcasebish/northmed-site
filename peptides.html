⚠️ Important Notice Regarding Peptide Therapies

Health Canada issued a public advisory in April 2026 warning Canadians about unauthorized injectable peptide drugs. Many compounds commonly discussed in longevity and wellness contexts — including BPC-157, TB-500, CJC-1295, ipamorelin, epithalon, semax, and DSIP — are not currently authorized for sale in Canada and have been seized by Health Canada from Canadian clinics.

Corbin Care does not facilitate access to unauthorized health products. Any discussion of longevity or wellness approaches at Corbin Care occurs exclusively within the context of a consultation with a licensed Canadian physician, who will advise on what treatments are legally authorized and clinically appropriate for your individual situation.
