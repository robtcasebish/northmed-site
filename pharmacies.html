Our Pharmacy Partners

Canadian pharmacies, Canadian standards

Every medication dispensed through Corbin Care comes from a Health Canada registered Canadian compounding pharmacy that meets our stringent quality requirements.

Our Standards

Quality you can count on

🏥

Health Canada Registered

All partner pharmacies are registered with Health Canada and licensed by their provincial pharmacy regulatory authority. No exceptions.

🧪

Third-Party Testing

Compounded medications undergo independent potency and sterility testing. Results are reviewed by our medical team before onboarding any pharmacy partner.

🌡️

Cold Chain Compliance

Temperature-sensitive medications are shipped with validated cold chain packaging and monitoring to ensure potency on arrival.

📦

Discreet Packaging

All shipments arrive in unmarked, tamper-evident packaging. No product names or medical information visible on the outside of any package.

📋

Lot Tracking

Every dispensed medication is lot-tracked from preparation to delivery. In the rare event of a quality issue, we can trace and notify all affected patients within 24 hours.

🚀

Fast Nationwide Shipping

Our pharmacy network is distributed across Canada to minimize shipping times. Most patients receive their medication within 3 to 5 business days of prescription approval.

How It Works

From prescription to your door

  • Physician approves prescription in our secure system
  • Prescription routed to nearest qualified pharmacy
  • Pharmacy prepares and quality-checks medication
  • Packaged discreetly with shipping label
  • Tracking number sent to patient via portal
  • Delivered in 3 to 5 business days

Compounding vs. Brand Name

Compounded medications contain the same active pharmaceutical ingredient as branded products, prepared by licensed pharmacists at our pharmacy partners. They are not interchangeable with Health Canada approved finished products and are prepared for specific patients under physician prescription.

Your physician will discuss both options and help you choose the most appropriate approach for your clinical situation and budget.
